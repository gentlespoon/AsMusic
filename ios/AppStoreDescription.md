# Description

AsMusic is a clean, modern player for Navidrome and Subsonic.
Stream your personal library anywhere, then download albums, playlists, and favorites for offline listening.
Built for simplicity, AsMusic helps you enjoy your collection without clutter.

AsMusic requires a self-hosted Navidrome or Subsonic server to function.
This app does not provide any music hosting service.

---

Features:

- Multiple servers, accounts, and libraries
- Offline downloads for albums, playlists, and favorites
- Sleep timer
- Star, favorite, and like tracks

WIP:

- Cross-library listening
- CarPlay support

---

AsMusic is currently optimized around a focused listening experience, with more customization planned in future updates.
